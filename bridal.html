Purpose of Convention

This portfolio was developed for the Association of Bridal Consultants' Annual Convention. The Bridal Collective Annual Convention is the premier wedding industry convention where wedding planners and industry professionals gather to turn wedding visions into reality. With a variety of workshops, trade shows, and keynote speakers, the purpose of this convention is to provide a fantastic opportunity to meet and network with leading suppliers, acquire insightful knowledge, and learn about the newest trends in the wedding industry. With the convention being held in Savannah, Georgia, attendees will get the opportunity to see local wedding venues and local vendors for inspiration. This charming city is home to The Westin Savannah Harbor Golf Resort & Spa, where our convention will be held this year.
